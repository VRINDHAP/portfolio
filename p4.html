Stampede Predictor is a real-time crowd monitoring solution developed during the HACKHAZARDS '25 hackathon organized by The NAMESPACE Community. It aims to enhance public safety by analyzing crowd density and predicting potential stampede risks in real time. I contributed as the frontend developer, focusing on building a responsive and user-friendly interface. The project was submitted via Devfolio with a demo video, and it was recognized among the Top 15 teams in the Fluvio track, validating its innovative approach and real-world impact.
